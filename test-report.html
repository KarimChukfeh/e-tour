📋 Executive Summary

What is ETour Protocol?

ETour is a decentralized tournament platform that runs entirely on the blockchain. Players can compete in various games (TicTacToe, Chess, Connect Four) in structured tournaments with automated prize distribution. Think of it as an on-chain esports platform where everything is transparent and trustless.

Test Suite Status:

✅ Excellent health at 97.7% pass rate - The system is highly reliable with only 5 minor issues remaining out of 222 tests.

What We Test:

  • 🎮 Game Logic: Rules enforcement, win detection, move validation
  • 🏆 Tournament Flow: Player enrollment, bracket generation, round progression
  • 💰 Prize Distribution: Fair allocation based on placement (1st = 50%, 2nd = 25%, etc.)
  • ⏱️ Timeout System: Anti-stalling measures with 3-level escalation
  • 📊 Leaderboards: Player stats, earnings tracking, rankings
  • 🔒 Security: Access control, invalid operation prevention, edge cases

⏱️ Match Escalation (Anti-Stalling System) 4 tests • 2 contract bugs ▼
What is Match Escalation?
A 3-level system to prevent players from stalling tournaments. If a player doesn't move in time:
Level 1: Opponent claims timeout win
Level 2: Advanced players force eliminate both stalled players
Level 3: External players can replace stalled players and take their slot
Escalation Timeline
Level 1 - Timeout Claim (60s)
Opponent can claim victory after move timeout
Level 2 - Force Elimination (+120s)
Advanced player eliminates both stalled players
Level 3 - Slot Replacement (+180s)
External player claims slot and advances
