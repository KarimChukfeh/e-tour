⚡ Match Escalation System 11 PASSING 1 FAILING 10+ PENDING

Three-Level Stall Recovery Mechanism: Comprehensive testing of the escalation system designed to ensure matches never permanently stall. L1 (opponent timeout claim after 5 minutes) allows the waiting player to claim victory. L2 (advanced player force eliminate after L1 + 2 minutes) lets already-advanced players in later rounds force eliminate stalled players. L3 (external replacement after L1 + 4 minutes) allows any external player to replace the stalled player and continue the tournament. Core L2 and L3 functionality now working! 91.7% tests passing.

⛽ Gas Cost Analysis & Economic Feasibility

🎯 Executive Summary: Economically Viable on Layer-2 Networks

The ETour protocol has been rigorously tested for gas efficiency and demonstrates strong economic viability on Layer-2 networks. Maximum capacity gas testing with 224 concurrent ConnectFour players shows an average cost of $0.07 per player at typical L2 gas prices (0.05 gwei). Regular gameplay moves average 80k gas (~$0.024 at 0.1 gwei), making sustained gameplay affordable. The highest cost operation occurs when a player triggers tournament auto-start (up to 3.9M gas for 16-player tournaments), but this is a one-time initialization cost distributed across all participants.
